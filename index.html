About The Book

Hi there! These are exciting times to be a Christian and to come into the knowledge of God’s plan and purpose for you. With that in mind, we bring you the First Edition of your Beautiful Today.

It is timely and erudite. It is packaged to enrich you for spiritual and emotional growth while delivering profound, practical, and principle-centered approaches to life

Introducing

This book will not just deliver to you salient points for living. It will lead you to discover a path to personal growth and finding meaning in life

You will find the book to be a comingling of poetic and conteporary choice arrangement yet driving its message home to your heart

Thinking is free. You don't pay to dream or to think big. Your mind is your winning ticket.
LIVING YOUR BEST LIFE NOW!

this is your reality now as a new creation. You have all you need to succeed.

This life you have now is one of the miracles every man on earth ought to experience. And you have received it freely.

Go about your business knowing you are blessed, not laboring to be blessed.

BOUNCE BACK!

The ability to bounce back from a major setback is what makes the difference between winning and losing

You will never hear stories of great achievements without a sprinkle of losses.

God loves you and his grace is your safety net to land on. You can recover from that fall. It’s time to bounce back. Be determined

THE GOD-KIND

The life of God, Zoe, which you received when you gave your heart to Christ is no ordinary life.

You have the same abiliities like Jesus. He healed the sick, so can you

You see, the life of God is in you; it will teach you and develop your spirit. All you have to do is pay attention to it daily and live in the consciousness of it.

WHOLENESS

A state of being perfectly well in our spirit, soul and body

Since, wholeness is the prosperity of the body, soul and spirit, that is what we should enjoy as believers

Don’t settle for less in life either when all things are yours. Enjoy wholeness.

God’s desire for us can be summed up in one word, 'Wholeness'
